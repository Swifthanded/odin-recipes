Salt Water

Description

This is simple 4-step recipe to creating salt water.

Ingredients

  • Drinking Water
  • Table Salt
  • Measuring Cup
  • Measuring Spoon (Teaspoon)
  • Stirring Spoon

Steps

  1. Fill Measuring Cup with 8 ounces of Drinking Water.
  2. Use Measuring Spoon to measure 1 Teaspoon of Table Salt.
  3. Pour Table Salt into the Measuring Cup containing Drinking Water and Stir until dissolved.
  4. Use Salt Water according to task's needs.
