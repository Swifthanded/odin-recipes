Sugar Water

Description

This is simple 4-step recipe to creating sugar water.

Ingredients

  • Drinking Water
  • White Sugar
  • Measuring Cup
  • Measuring Spoon (Teaspoon)
  • Stirring Spoon

Steps

  1. Fill Measuring Cup with 8 ounces of Drinking Water.
  2. Use Measuring Spoon to measure 1 Teaspoon of White Sugar.
  3. Pour White Sugar into the Measuring Cup containing Drinking Water and Stir until dissolved.
  4. Use Sugar Water according to task's needs.
